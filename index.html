About

Alisa has been teaching violin and piano for over 15 years. Her lesson plans are inspired by the student, her own education, and her 18 years performing as a professional musician. Primarily a classical violinist with a Bachelor's degree in violin performance, Alisa has been improvising and composing in bands her whole life.


Studio located at 221 Linden Ave, Riverton, NJ

Policy

Studying with Alisa is a monthly commitment. She offers in-home lessons, limited in-studio lessons, and yearly studio recitals. The student will learn to read music, learn to play alongside others and will showcase their work in a yearly recital.

Lessons

Violin


Violin lessons with Alisa are tailored to the students' interests and goals in music. Alisa bases her lessons classically to develop a strong technical foundation while approaching her lesson plan with the student’s life and musical interests in mind. Students will learn to read music, gain technique, play with others and will showcase what they have learned in a yearly recital. Ready to achieve fluency on the violin? Contact me to book violin lessons.


"I prefer to teach the Sassmannshaus method, which is popular in Germany for the young violinists. As they grow in their technique, we will begin to introduce classic etudes and orchestral repertoire that can win a student an audition or provide technical fluency to be able to just PLAY." - Alisa C.


Piano


Piano is the best introductory instrument in music and beautifully translates into any other instrument the student grows into. Students will learn to read sheet music/charts, gain technique, play with others and will showcase what they have learned in a yearly recital. Interested in starting to learn the musical "language"? Contact me to book piano lessons.
